Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza features a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, and gooey mozzarella cheese. Customize with your favorite toppings for a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • ½ cup warm water
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1½ cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Mix flour, yeast, and water to form a dough. Let it rise for 1 hour.

  2. Step 2

    Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out the dough and spread tomato sauce on top.

  3. Step 3

    Add cheese and toppings, then bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
