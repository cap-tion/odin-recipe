Burger

Description

A classic homemade burger made with juicy beef patties, fresh lettuce, tomato, and melted cheese in a toasted bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 4 slices of cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then shape into patties.

  2. Step 2

    Grill or pan-fry patties for about 4-5 minutes per side until cooked through.

  3. Step 3

    Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and patty in a bun.
